Your Grace

They cast me out into the night.
I know not where and cannot fight.
What benefits I did possess
were snatched away with my address.

I do not need to reason why.
The fact remains that I will die.
Left alone in hidden fire
(Not a thing that I desire).

Who would consider saving me-
A wretch, a wreck identity?
Lord, take pity on my behalf.
Save me from the enemy wrath.

Drop your hand into the flame.
Quench the heat and make it tame.
My hope rests solely in your grace
and at love's mention, see your face.

-Samuel
